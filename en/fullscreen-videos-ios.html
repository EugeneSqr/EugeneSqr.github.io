Fullscreen Videos In IOS

2017-11-17

How can I get HTML5 video in full screen in IOS?

It appeared harder than expected. Quick searching resulted in the following:

  1. webkitRequestFullScreen – a standard way for webkit browsers (Chrome, Safari). The problem is you don't find the method on IOS mobile devices.
  2. requestFullscreen – how about this, in accordance with W3C standard? Same thing.
  3. Let's see browser support for full screen API. It appears the API isn't supported by Safari in IOS. It is also the case for Chrome there.

What to do?

I found mentioning the webkitendfullscreen event here. A pair event webkitendfullscreen followed right after it. There had to be more... Finally this excellent article explained two key methods:

  • webkitEnterFullScreen
  • webkitExitFullScreen

Morale

The interesting thing in this story is that the main sources of information about web development – stackoverflow and MDN – didn't contain much info on the topic. One should not underestimate Apple's and Microsoft's documentation when it comes to their products.
